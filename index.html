Privacy Policy for Namaz Takip

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Namaz Takip (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Local Prayer Records: Stored locally on your device.
  • Location Data: City and coordinates stored locally for prayer time calculations.
  • Account Information: If you use Cloud Backup, we collect your email via Firebase Authentication.

3. Data Deletion & Account Closure

In accordance with Google Play's User Data policy, we provide clear paths for you to delete your data:

How to request deletion:

To delete your account and all associated cloud data (Prayer Records and Email), please send an email to aytacserce@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

Your request will be processed within 7 business days, and all your records stored in Firebase will be permanently removed.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for optional cloud backup and authentication. Data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.
