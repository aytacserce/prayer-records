Privacy Policy for Namaz Takip

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Namaz Takip (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

Our app is designed with a privacy-first, offline-first philosophy.

  • Local Prayer Records: All records of your daily and voluntary prayers are stored locally on your device.
  • Location Data: To provide accurate prayer times, we store your city name and coordinates (latitude/longitude) locally. We do not track your live movement.
  • Account Information: If you choose to enable the Cloud Backup feature, we collect your email address for authentication purposes via Firebase Authentication.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To Provide Services: Calculating prayer times based on your selected location.
  • Optional Cloud Sync: If opted-in, your prayer records are securely backed up to Firebase Firestore so you can restore them if you change devices.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to enhance app functionality:

  • Google Firebase: Used for optional cloud backup, data storage, and authentication. Data handled by Firebase is subject to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Aladhan API & Nominatim: Used to fetch prayer times and geocoding information. These services do not receive personally identifiable information (PII) from the App.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local Data: You can clear your local data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its cache in your device settings.
  • Cloud Data: If you have enabled Cloud Backup, you may request the deletion of your account and all associated cloud data by contacting us at the email provided below.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.
